248 vizualizações

The street Voluntários da Pátria is located in Botafogo, Rio de Janeiro. It starts right after Rua Humaitá and ends at Praia de Botafogo. It is one of the busiest streets in the neighborhood with vehicle traffic and a large flow of people circulating throughout the day and night. It is also known for having numerous commercial stores along its entire length.

The street is fully paved and it is also possible to find the options available, especially for those who like to be close to the hustle and bustle of a big city and have all kinds of services at their feet, without the need for a car or public transport.

Voluntários da Pátria connects with important streets in Botafogo, such as Conde de Irajá, Martins Ferreira, Real Grandeza, Matriz, São João Batista, Palmeiras, Sorocaba, Dona Mariana, Guilhermina Guinle, Dezenove de Fevereiro, Paulino Fernandes and Nelson Mandela.
